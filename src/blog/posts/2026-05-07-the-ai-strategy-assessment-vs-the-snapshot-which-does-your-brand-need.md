In the rapidly evolving landscape of AI-driven search, businesses often ask: "Where do I even start?" There are two ways to look at your digital visibility through the lens of an LLM, and understanding the difference is key to your growth.

**The AI Visibility Snapshot: The "Pulse Check"**

Think of the Snapshot as a high-level diagnostic. It answers the immediate question: how do AI models see my brand right now? It's a point-in-time look at whether you are being recommended for your core keywords and if your brand narrative is consistent across different platforms. It's fast, insightful, and perfect for identifying immediate red flags — and it's free.

**A Deeper Strategy Engagement: The "Deep Dive"**

While the Snapshot shows you the *what*, a deeper strategy engagement explains the *why* and the *how*. This looks at data pipelines (how your business data is being fed into the ecosystem), competitive gap analysis (why competitors are getting the "mention" over you), and a long-term roadmap to ensure your brand remains a primary reference point for AI models as they become the new gatekeepers of search. This is the work covered under the [GEO Retainer](/services/).
