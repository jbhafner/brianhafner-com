In the "old days" of digital marketing, mystery shopping meant checking your own storefront or calling your sales line. Today, the most important "shopper" your business will ever encounter isn't human—it's an AI agent.

## Looking through the "Eyes" of the AI

When a potential customer asks an AI model for a recommendation in your industry, that model performs a lightning-fast "mystery shop" of your entire digital presence. It evaluates:

1. **Authority:** Does the web agree that you are an expert?
2. **Sentiment:** What is the prevailing "mood" regarding your customer service?
3. **Accuracy:** Are your technical specs, pricing, or locations consistent everywhere?

## The Practical Way to Audit This Yourself

One of the most effective ways to understand this is to watch it happen in real time. By prompting an LLM to "act as a customer looking for [your service]," you can see exactly how it weighs your brand against others.

If the AI hesitates, hallucinates, or—worst of all—recommends a competitor, you have a visibility gap. Identifying these gaps is the first step toward fixing them. You aren't just optimizing for a search engine anymore; you are optimizing for a recommendation engine.
