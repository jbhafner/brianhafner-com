Frequently asked questions

How much does this cost?

The AI Visibility Snapshot is free. GEO Retainer and Lead-to-Revenue Tracking are scoped per project after a short discovery call — pricing depends on the size of your site and how your deals close.

How long does a Snapshot take to deliver?

Snapshots are delivered within 3 business days of request.

Who do you typically work with?

Service businesses whose sales close offline or through a sales conversation — quote requests, phone calls, consultations — rather than an online checkout.

What don't you do?

I'm not a full-service agency. I don't run paid social campaigns, build full e-commerce sites, or manage broad international SEO. The focus is AI Visibility, GEO, and attribution.
