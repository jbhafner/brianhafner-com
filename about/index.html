About

I'm Brian Hafner. I help service businesses get found in AI answers.

I'm Brian Hafner, an AI Visibility consultant helping service businesses get cited, recommended, and quoted by ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. Based in San Diego, I combine two decades of data and analytics experience with hands-on technical implementation — schema, entity clarity, and revenue attribution that proves the work is paying off.

Search is fragmenting. Brands built for citation win.

The traditional search bar is no longer the only front door. AI answer engines now summarize, recommend, and quote brands directly — often without a click-through at all. Businesses that structure their online presence to be clearly understood by AI models get recommended. Businesses that don't stay invisible, even if their traditional SEO looks fine.

That shift changes what "good marketing" means. It's no longer just about ranking — it's about being the answer an AI model is confident enough to cite by name.

From Tokyo trading floors to AI strategy.

For over two decades I've navigated the evolution of data — from building trading applications in Tokyo's financial sector, to managing six-figure monthly ad spends for modern brands, to a full-stack development certification at UC San Diego Extension. Each chapter reframes the same underlying skill: finding signal in ambiguous, high-stakes data and building systems that make it legible.

That analytical foundation is what I bring to AI Visibility work today — it's not a pivot, it's the same discipline applied to a new layer of search.
